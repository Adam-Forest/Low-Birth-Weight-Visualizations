1. Load requirements.txt
2. In Postgres create a new database: low-birth-weigh
3. Open jupyter notebook and run low_birth_weight.ipynb (change line: In [7] with your postgres user/password/port)
4. Run: $ python app.py (change lines 16-18 with your postgres user/password/port)
5. Open browser to http://localhost:5000/
Enjoy!
